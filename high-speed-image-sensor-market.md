<p><p>The high-speed image sensor market is primarily divided into digital and analog types. Digital image sensors convert light into digital signals, enabling high-resolution image capture and advanced processing capabilities, making them ideal for applications in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial automation. Analog image sensors, on the other hand, produce continuous electrical signals corresponding to the light intensity, catering to applications where real-time performance and lower power consumption are crucial. Both types play distinct roles in various imaging applications.</p></p>
