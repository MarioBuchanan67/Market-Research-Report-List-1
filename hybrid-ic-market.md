<p><p>The hybrid IC market is categorized into three main types: glass epoxy substrate, metal substrate, and other substrates. Glass epoxy substrates are favored for their lightweight and cost-effectiveness, offering good insulation properties for various electronic applications. Metal substrates, typically aluminum or copper, provide excellent thermal conductivity, making them ideal for high-power applications. Other substrates include ceramic and flexible materials, which cater to specialized needs in advanced electronics. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to specific industry requirements.</p></p>
