<p><p>The elastic sensor market encompasses various types, including pressure sensors and tension sensors. Tension sensors, on the other hand, monitor the tension or load applied to cables or structures, ensuring safety and reliability in construction and manufacturing. Both sensor types leverage elastic materials to convert mechanical changes into measurable electrical signals, enhancing precision across diverse industries.</p></p>
