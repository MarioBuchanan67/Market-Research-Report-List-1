<p><p>The Active Pixel Sensor (APS) market is segmented into various types, including Buried Photodiode structures, Selection Transistors, and MOS Amplifiers. Buried Photodiode structures enhance sensitivity and reduce noise, making them ideal for high-performance imaging applications. Selection Transistors enable pixel selection and readout, optimizing overall sensor efficiency. MOS Amplifiers serve to amplify the signal from each pixel, improving image quality and dynamic range. These components collectively enhance the performance and functionality of APS in diverse imaging technologies.</p></p>
