<p><p>The probe card market consists of several key types, each suited for different semiconductor testing needs. Cantilever probe cards feature a single row of probes for high-density testing, offering excellent performance for wafer-level test applications. Vertical probe cards utilize a vertical arrangement of probes for enhanced accessibility and optimized signal integrity, ideal for high-frequency testing. MEMS probe cards leverage micro-electromechanical systems technology for precision and reliability, tackling miniaturized devices with reduced contact forces and higher density interconnects.</p></p>
